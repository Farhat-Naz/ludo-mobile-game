# Privacy Policy for Ludo Game

**Last Updated**: December 25, 2024

## Introduction

Ludo Game ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.**

Ludo Game is designed to respect your privacy completely:

### Data We DO NOT Collect:
- ❌ No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No device identifiers
- ❌ No usage analytics
- ❌ No crash reports
- ❌ No gameplay statistics
- ❌ No advertising data
- ❌ No cookies or tracking technologies

### What the App Does:
- ✅ Works completely offline
- ✅ Stores game state locally on your device only
- ✅ No internet connection required
- ✅ No third-party services or SDKs
- ✅ No ads or analytics

## Local Data Storage

The app stores minimal game state locally on your device:
- Current game progress (if you pause a game)
- User preferences (sound/haptic settings, if implemented)

**This data:**
- Never leaves your device
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Is not accessible to us or any third parties
- Cannot identify you personally

## Permissions

The app requires **NO special permissions**.

Optional features:
- **Haptic Feedback**: Uses device vibration API (no permission required)
- **Audio**: Plays sound effects locally (no permission required)

## Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No social media integration
- No cloud storage or backup services
- No crash reporting tools

## Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):
- We do not collect any information from anyone, including children
- The app is safe for all ages
- No in-app purchases or external links
- No communication features

## Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure. All game state is stored locally on your device using standard Android secure storage.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

If we ever decide to collect any data, we will:
1. Update this privacy policy
2. Request your explicit consent through the app
3. Provide clear opt-out options

- **Right to Access**: No data collected to access
- **Right to Deletion**: No data collected to delete
